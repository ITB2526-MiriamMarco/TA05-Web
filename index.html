CYBERSECURITY WEB PAGE INFORMATION

This website is dedicated to exploring the core principles and practices of **Cybersecurity**. Cybersecurity is the practice of defending computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks, and data from malicious attacks. It is essential in today's digital world to protect sensitive information from theft and damage.

Our focus includes **Network Security**, which protects the internal network from intruders; **Application Security**, which ensures software and devices are free of threats; and **Information Security**, which protects the integrity and privacy of data, both in storage and transit. Implementing strong **access controls**, using **encryption**, and maintaining regular **software updates** are key components of a robust security posture.

We aim to educate users about common threats like **malware**, **phishing**, and **ransomware**, and provide actionable insights into best defensive strategies. Stay informed, stay secure.
