Cybersecurity Tasks: Overview

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. These attacks are usually aimed at accessing, changing, or destroying sensitive information; extorting money from users; or interrupting normal business processes.

Key Activities:

  • **Risk Analysis:** Identifying vulnerabilities and threats in the system.
  • **Threat Monitoring:** Using tools like SIEM to monitor network traffic for suspicious activity.
  • **Implementing Defenses:** Deploying firewalls, antivirus, and intrusion prevention systems.
  • **Incident Response:** Handling security breaches, containing the damage, and recovering affected systems.
  • **Security Audits:** Regularly testing systems (e.g., penetration testing) to find weaknesses.
  • **User Education:** Training employees on security protocols (phishing, password hygiene).
